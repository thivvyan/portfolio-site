Global Hack '19 Extended

Global Runner Up

The Global Flutter Community and Google, jointly intiated the first ever global flutter hackothan which was open for all the participants world wide. Even though I'm not a developer , I participated in the hackothan playing the role of a business anlayst in my team. I participated in the hackothan representing Informatics Institute of Technology and the Colombo Flutter Community.. There were two rounds of selections in the hackothan, in the first round we were placed as the country level winners and represented Sri Lanka in the final round where we were announced as the Global Runner Up for the first ever flutter hackothan. I participated in this competition along with my friends akassharjun , Isala and Suvin.

Innovation Awardee

Citizen's Youth Summit

The Citizen's Youth Summit was organised by Citizens a youth led movement focusing on Youth Empowerment and Peacebuilding. Their purpose is to empower global youth leaders to shape the future for the better. We are a trusted platform for the youth of global society to integrate and aggregate their efforts to improve the state of the world. I applied for the summit as a participant in one of their competitions where I got selected as one of the 10 innovation awardees out of 180 delegates who participated in the summit. I presented white baord which was a smart e - learning platform which focuses on teritary education.

Student Employability Award 2021

University of Westminster

Westminster Employability Award is an employability achievement award supporting your career and personal development by formally recognising the extra-curricular activities you complete during your time at the University.Nowadays, employers want much more than just graduates with a degree. They value individuals who have invested in their personal and professional development and have gained skills through various activities. You will get recognition for the extra-curricular activities you do on and off-campus and will be able to identify and evidence the skills you have developed to potential employers. It was a pleasure to be one of the first few overseas students from Sri Lanka to win the title of Employability Gold Award.

Yarl Geek Challenge 2020

Finalists

Yarl Geek Challenge is a premier technology competition open to all youth. The intent of the competition is to nurture IT skills, innovation and creativity, fostering a spirit of entrepreneurship among the entrants. I participated as a team along with my friends to showcase our business idea "Home Brands". Home Brands is a platform that connects businesses with potential customers. My team members were Akasharjun Shanmugarajah, Suresh Michael , Dilshan Manathunge and Visal Rajapakse

Metamorphosis 2020

Finalists

Metamorphosis ’20 is an initiative of Incoming Global Entrepreneur/Talent function together with Partnership Development function of AIESEC in University of Sri Jayewardenepura to bring out the talent of the Sri Lankan youth, by providing them the stage to showcase their innovations and ground breaking ideas, which can be a support in the fast-paced world. Our team "Home Brands" represented Informatics Institute of Technology in the competition and was able to make it to the top 5 finalists. My team members were Suresh Michael , Akassharjun Shanmugarajah , Visal Rajapakse and Dilshan Manathunge.
